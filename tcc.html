The Crazy Computer


Now THIS is a mad machine...


Released on : 2019/04/14 - Role in the game : Made everything (bar standard Scratch assets and SFX)

Well, THAT's a fine mess... Scratchy (aka the Scratch Cat) was about to turn on his computer when he got a nasty surprise... It was infected! And so he got sucked inside his own computer!

Control Scratchy in a text-based adventure to get out of his own computer!

This game was started during a coding event in June 2018. It was my first project ever, and I learned how to write a story, as well as the bases of coding, during this game's development. It's available in both French and English, so you can test it here :

  • Original French version
  • English translation

A sequel for this game was originally planned. Its development had started a few days after this game's completion, but it was never finished. After a year of development of the cancelled sequel, I eventually got tired of Scratch and eventually turned away from it. You can test the unfinished project here (beware, it was NOT translated).
