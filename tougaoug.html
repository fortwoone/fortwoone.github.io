Tougaoug Green


The climate change is an actual thing!


Released on: 2023/12/08 - Role: Coded the Tetris game found on the 404 page


During the Nuit de l'Info 2023 (an event challenging students to code a website about a certain subject while trying to clear some secondary challenges on the same website (e.g. find a creative way to compress images)), we had to work in teams (in my case, the TougaougWeb team was compounded of 12 people).
I was tasked with the completion of both the "Rhythm Game" and "404 Tetris" challenges, which I merged into one Tetris game. I managed to nab the third place for the "Rhythm Game" challenge for my team.
Two versions of this game currently exist:

  • The original version, hosted on the website made during the event (in French) (in case the former link doesn't work anymore, please head here)
  • An updated version, maintained and less buggy (in English)
