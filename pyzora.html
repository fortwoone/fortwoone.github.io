PyZora


A password decoder/generator library for The Legend of Zelda: Oracle of Ages/Seasons.

Released on : 2023/11/30 (GitHub source) / 2023/12/04 (PyPI) - Role: Library Author


This Python library offers flexible control over The Legend of Zelda: Oracle of Seasons/Ages secret passwords! It was created as a training project, and bases itself on ZoraSharp, which in turn bases itself on Paul D. Shoener III's work on the original password generator made in 2001 (available on the Wayback Machine due to his website being archived).

You can head here to check the source code, or click this link to read the docs. You can also check the library's page on PyPI.
